About EcoFresh Solutions

EcoFresh Solutions is an innovative startup based in Peka, Leribe, Lesotho, focused on providing eco-friendly and sustainable cleaning products. Our goal is to reduce environmental pollution while promoting healthier lifestlyes in homes and businesses.

Our Story

EcoFresh Solutions was founded on the 27th January 2026, it began as a small entrepreneurial idea driven by the need to repclace harmful chemical-based cleaning products with safer, natural alternatives.
Recognizing the growing concern about environmental sustainability, we developed products that are both effective and environmentally responsible.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, high-quality, and eco-friendly products that protect both people and the planet. We are committed to reducing the use of toxic chemicals in everyday cleaning.

Our Vision

To become a leading global brand in eco-friendly household and cleaning solutions, known for innovation, sustainability, and customer trust.

Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: We prioritize environmentally safe practices.
  • Innovation: We continuosly improve our produts.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customer come first.
  • Integrity: We operate honestly and responsibly.
  • Quality: We ensure high standards in every product.

Our Impact

EcoFresh Solutions is committed to reducing environmental waste by using recyclable packaging and promoting sustainable consumption. We aim to make a positive impact in our community and beyond.
